Languages We Offer

Japanese

Our Japanese language program follows the JLPT (Japanese Language Proficiency Test) structure:

  • N5: Basic grammar, vocabulary, simple sentence formation, and fundamental kanji.
  • N4: Improved reading comprehension, grammar, and conversational skills.
  • N3: Intermediate level with advanced sentence structures and listening skills.

Korean

Our Korean courses are based on the TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean):

  • TOPIK I: (Level 1 & 2) Basic phrases, greetings, and simple sentences for daily communication.
  • TOPIK II: (Levels 3-6) Intermediate to advanced fluency, comprehension of complex texts, and academic/professional usage.

Chinese (Mandarin)

We offer Mandarin courses aligned with the HSK (Hanyu Shuiping Kaoshi) test:

  • HSK 1-2: Basic Chinese characters, greetings, and simple expressions.
  • HSK 3-4: Intermediate fluency with expanded vocabulary and structured sentences.
  • HSK 5-6: Advanced communication for business, academics, and professional use.

English

Our English courses focus on comprehensive language skills and test preparation:

  • IELTS & TOEFL Preparation: Intensive training for academic and professional certification.
  • General English: Covers grammar, vocabulary, speaking, listening, reading, and writing from beginner to advanced levels.
